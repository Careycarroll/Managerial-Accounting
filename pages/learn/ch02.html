Tool 4 of 4

The Unit Cost Trap

Unit costs include both variable and fixed components. Using a unit cost calculated at one volume to predict costs at a different volume leads to serious errors. See it happen.

Item Base Volume Predicted Volume
⚠️
The Trap

The Rule: Always separate fixed and variable costs. Calculate total costs as Total Fixed + (Variable per unit × Units). Only convert to unit cost at the end, if needed for a specific decision.
